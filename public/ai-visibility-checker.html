Why Your Business Might Not Show Up

If ChatGPT or Google AI Overviews recommends competitors instead of your business, there are usually five fixable reasons:

  1. AI crawlers are blocked. Default robots.txt rules block GPTBot, ClaudeBot, and PerplexityBot on most WordPress, Wix, and Squarespace sites.
  2. No schema markup. Pages with correct JSON-LD schema are 3.6× more likely to appear in Google AI Overviews (Google, 2024).
  3. No entity authority. AI models recommend businesses they've seen mentioned consistently. No mentions = no recommendation.
  4. Content reads like marketing copy. AI extracts answer-first, fact-rich passages — not "Welcome to our company" intros.
  5. Missing E-E-A-T signals. Anonymous businesses score poorly. Named team, credentials, and Person schema all matter.
